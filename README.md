# 🚗 Car Sales Dashboard | Power BI

## 📌 Project Overview
This project presents a **comprehensive Car Sales Dashboard** developed using **Power BI** to analyze, monitor, and evaluate car sales performance across multiple dimensions such as time, region, body style, color, and company.

The dashboard transforms raw sales data into **actionable insights**, enabling business users to track performance, identify growth opportunities, and make **data-driven strategic decisions**.

---
## 🛠 Tools & Technologies
- **Power BI** – Dashboard & reporting  
- **DAX** – Measures and KPI calculations  
- **Excel / CSV** – Data source  
- **Data Modeling** – Relationships & filters  

---
## 🎯 Business Objective
The primary objective of this project is to:
- Provide a **single source of truth** for sales performance
- Track **key KPIs** like sales revenue, average price, and cars sold
- Compare current performance with **previous year benchmarks**
- Identify **top-performing and underperforming segments**
- Support management in **strategic planning and decision-making**

---

## 🧩 Business Problem
The car dealership operates across multiple regions and sells various car models with different body styles and colors.  
However, the business faced challenges such as:
- Difficulty in tracking **overall sales performance**
- Lack of visibility into **weekly and yearly sales trends**
- No clear comparison between **current and previous year performance**
- Limited understanding of **which body styles, colors, and regions drive revenue**
- Manual reporting that was **time-consuming and inefficient**

---

## ✅ Business Solution
This Power BI dashboard solves the above problems by:
- Centralizing all sales data into an **interactive dashboard**
- Automating KPI calculations using **DAX**
- Providing both **high-level summary** and **detailed transaction views**
- Allowing users to filter data dynamically by body style, dealer, engine, and transmission

---

## 📊 Key KPIs Implemented

### 🔹 Sales Performance
- Year-to-Date (YTD) Total Sales  
- Month-to-Date (MTD) Total Sales  
- Year-over-Year (YOY) Growth  
- Difference between YTD and Previous YTD (PTYD)

### 🔹 Pricing Analysis
- YTD & MTD Average Price  
- YOY Change in Average Price  
- Price comparison with previous year

### 🔹 Sales Volume
- YTD & MTD Cars Sold  
- YOY Growth in Cars Sold  
- Volume comparison with PTYD

---

## 📈 Visualizations & Features

- **Weekly YTD Sales Trend** – Identifies seasonality and sales spikes  
- **Sales by Body Style (Pie Chart)** – Highlights customer preferences  
- **Sales by Color (Pie Chart)** – Helps optimize inventory planning  
- **Cars Sold by Dealer Region (Map)** – Reveals regional performance gaps  
- **Company-wise Sales Grid** – Compares brand contribution and dominance  
- **Detailed Sales Table** – Enables drill-down to individual transactions  

---

## 🔍 Key Business Insights Derived

- 📈 **Strong YTD sales growth** indicates healthy business performance compared to the previous year  
- 🚘 **SUVs and Sedans** contribute the highest share of total sales, showing customer preference  
- 🎨 **Pale White and Black** are the most sold car colors, helping inventory optimization  
- 🌍 Certain dealer regions consistently outperform others, enabling **region-specific sales strategies**  
- 🏭 A few companies contribute a **major portion of total revenue**, highlighting key brand partnerships  
- 💰 Despite sales growth, **average price shows slight fluctuation**, indicating competitive pricing or discounts  

---

## 🧠 Business Impact
This dashboard enables the business to:
- Make **faster and smarter decisions**
- Identify **revenue-driving segments**
- Optimize **inventory and pricing strategies**
- Improve **regional and dealer-level performance**
- Reduce dependency on manual reports
